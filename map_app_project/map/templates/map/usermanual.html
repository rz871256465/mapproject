User Manual: Map Tasks Application

Welcome to the Map Quest app!

This user manual provides guidance on how to effectively navigate and use the Map Tasks application. Our platform enables researchers to conduct controlled experiments that assess problem-solving and communication skills among participants. Below, you will find instructions tailored for participants and researchers.

For Participants:

Getting Started:

URL: https://map-interaction-platform-164b99c52895.herokuapp.com/

Logging In:

Navigate to the application login page. Enter your username and password provided by your administrator. Click "Log In" to access.

If you don't have an account, you'll need to register

And then you will come to Registration Page

Performing Tasks:

Roles:

Instruction Givers will see the complete map with routes and need to guide their partner. Followers will rely on instructions from their partner to navigate their map, which has limited visibility.

Giver views:

Follower views:

Using the Chat Panel:

Communicate with your partner using the chat panel. Ensure clarity and precision in your instructions or queries.

Completing the Task:

Follow the instructions to complete the navigation challenge.

Givers: Guide your partner accurately.

Followers: Indicate your progress and any confusion promptly.

Logging Out:

Click "Logout" at the top-right corner when you have completed your tasks.
